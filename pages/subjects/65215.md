<p>(April 25, 1805 – June 16, 1876) Edward Augustus Holyoke Hemenway was an American self-made merchant from Boston, Massachusetts. Married to archeologist philanthropist, Mary Tilton Hemenway. Their children were Charlotte Augusta Hemenway (1841-1865), Alice, (1862-1862), Edith (~1851-1904), and Amy Hemenway (1848-1911) and Agustus Hemenway Jr. (1853–1931), Buried in lot 1463.</p>
