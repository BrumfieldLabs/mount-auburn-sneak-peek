<h3><a name="page-1484752">Letter: Charles Lanman, Legation of Japan, to Mount Auburn Superintendent, 1882</a></h3>
<div class="page-content">
<p>Subject: Letterhead [pencil]</p>
<p>Legation of Japan,<span class='line-break'> </span>Washington.</p>
<p>May 10^th^, 1882</p>
<p>Dear Sir:</p>
<p>This Legation would be <span class='line-break'> </span>glad to receive any printed<span class='line-break'> </span>document that you have,<span class='line-break'> </span>which will give the history &amp;<span class='line-break'> </span>management of the Mount<span class='line-break'> </span>Auburn Cemetery &amp; be greatly<span class='line-break'> </span>obliged for the same.</p>
<p>Very Respectfully yours</p>
<p>Charles Lanman<span class='line-break'> </span>for the [Chapter? ...]</p>
<p>To the Superintendent<span class='line-break'> </span>Mount Auburn Cemetery,<span class='line-break'> </span>Mass.</p>
