<p>Dear Sirs, <span class='line-break'> </span>Yours of the 14th reeived and I accept your offer of <span class='line-break'> </span>1000 maps like the sample you sent and five dollars  for the back <span class='line-break'> </span>cover of the forthcoming edition of the Mt. Auburn Guide. Kindly<span class='line-break'> </span>send in the copy for the changes on the cover and order your maps<span class='line-break'> </span>so that we may get them out without delay. <span class='line-break'> </span>Very truly yours, <span class='line-break'> </span>Moses King<span class='line-break'> </span></p>
